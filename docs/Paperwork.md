# Iskhod Outpost – Requests Console Paperwork

This page describes the paperwork available from **Requests Consoles** on Iskhod Outpost. Forms are printed from the console and can be filled in with a pen. The templates use special notation that is interpreted when the form is printed or when you write on it.

---

## Notation used in forms

| Notation | Meaning |
|----------|--------|
| **`[field]`** | A **writable box**. When the form is printed, this appears as an empty area that can be written in later with a pen. Use for fill-in blanks (names, dates, details, etc.). |
| **`[sign]`** | **Printer’s name**. When the form is printed, this is replaced with the **name of the person who printed the form** (from their ID card, or their character name if no ID). Use for “signed by” lines so the signatory is recorded automatically. |
| **`[date]`** | Replaced with the **current station date** when the form is printed. |
| **`[time]`** | Replaced with the **current station time** when the form is printed. |

Forms are defined in `code/game/machinery/requests_console_paperwork.dm`. This wiki matches that file.

## Where to print forms

Use any **Requests Console** (e.g. at department desks). Open the console, choose **Print Paperwork**, pick a category, then select the form to print. The form is created at your feet; use a pen to fill in any `[field]` areas. `[sign]`, `[date]`, and `[time]` are already filled when the form is printed.
