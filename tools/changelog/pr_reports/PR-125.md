# PR #125: Destroying clothing will drop accessories, items in pockets, on the ground

- **Author:** du-top
- **Link:** https://github.com/epic-new-soj-thing/Sojourn-Iskhod/pull/125

## About The Pull Request
<details>
<summary>About The Pull Request</summary>
<hr>

- Removes the references to clothes' health values, which are not used.
 
 - Boot knives will be dropped on the floor if boots are dissolved by acid or otherwise destroyed
 
 - Accessories on clothing will be dropped on the floor if the clothes they are attached to are dissolved by acid or otherwise destroyed
 
 - Items in oversuit pockets (labcoat, for example) will be dropped on the floor if the suit is dissolved by acid or otherwise destroyed
 
 - When hit with acid, each layer (one of three) of clothing, from outside to inside, should protect the clothing of a body part one layer below. Which does NOT work as well as I hoped for. A labcoat will cover chest and arms, but not the legs, so the jumpsuit is not protected and will dissolve. Protection works in a way that the amount of acid sprayed is used up per each dissolving of a clothing piece. On one hit your jumpsuit might dissolve, but your boots remain, the next hit could cost them, too.

- Yes, the third layer from the outside is the underwear layer. This is not a hidden ERP change. Don't know about you, but _my_ undies aren't made from thick rubber.

This change is a copy of https://github.com/sojourn-13/sojourn-station/pull/6007 on Sojourn, also done by me.
