# PR #79: Remove Pop Culture References from Cigarettes

- **Author:** Smokingsquirrel
- **Link:** https://github.com/epic-new-soj-thing/Sojourn-Iskhod/pull/79

## About The Pull Request
<details>
<summary>About The Pull Request</summary>
<hr>

Removes the pop culture references I could find.

Ishimuras from Dead Space - Zipangu Special Packet (Zipangu is the medieval name for Japan to keep the japanese naming)
Tannhauser Gate from BladeRunner - Tannenberg (Place of two major battles that had a major impact in history to keep german naming)
Brouzouf Message from Brouzouf - While Brouzouf is french already, it is changed to "Clope" cigarette, which is french slang for "Cigarette". You now smoke a "Cigarette" Cigarette
Shodans from System Shock - MindFusion (keeping it in line with the flavor text)
TOHA Heavy Industries from Tsotuma Nihei - Sanperitor Export (To keep the theme of industrial cigarettes)
Fortress Cigarettes from Team Fortress - Bastion Cigarettes
